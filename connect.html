Problem.

Refocusing the Company.

When our Chief Product Officer Rob Reynold joined the company, he outlined a three-front approach to focus the company’s efforts over the next two years. Those fronts were robust course authoring, rich data analytics, and enhance our social layer. I was proud to be lead designer on course authoring and lead designer on what would become communities.

Benefits

  3. Build interest around courses not enrolled in
  4. Build a sense of community for distance learners
  5. Bridging the Gap Between Professionals and Students

In the fall of last year, I was tasked to research, design, and refine a large scale approach to communities on our platform.

Process.

Bi-Daily Check-ins

At the start of the project there was a ton of divergent exercises, such as random entry to create divergent ideas. Toward the end we needed a way to bring the work together and started meeting at lunch and the end of the day to review each other's ideas. This allowed us to converge on similar interaction patterns and provide valuable input on the challenges we were facing.
